Instructions

Welcome to Piano Hero

In Piano Hero, the player is given a virtual piano, with which, they must follow along with the tabs on the screen to one the popular song choices from the menu!

These tabs will be listed as keys you must hit on your keyboard, if you miss a key, it will be counted against you, but if you get at least 70% you pass!

In this game, case sensitivity DOES matter. Lowercase letters stand for regular notes, where as uppercase represents sharp notes

This game also comes with a free play option! If you want to use this device to learn piano with your own notes, hit free play. You have the button on your keyboard to switch to ACTUAL music notes so that you know what each keyboard key represents

Please note, don't get characters such as the bracket ] and the letter Jmixed up, they are different!

Have fun!
